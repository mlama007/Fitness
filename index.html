About Me


Personal Trainer

Welcome to my page I’m Luis Matamoros and I’m currently a senior at Florida State University studying Exercise Physiology on the Pre-Physical Therapy route. I’ve been an athlete ever since I could remember playing competitive soccer most of my life and in 2011 I began my fitness journey. I have a huge passion for everything health and fitness related, and I love to learn the science of how training, nutrition, and physiology all come into play to maximize individual health and fitness goals. I’m working towards one day becoming a Doctor of Physical Therapy so that I can help people live a long healthy life and have optimal physical functions.

Education / Certification

  • Personal Trainer for 2 years at the YMCA, currently an online fitness coach
  • Exercise Physiology / Pre-PT at FSU
